What We Do Best

End-to-end cardiovascular trial expertise, from first protocol draft to final regulatory submission.

Clinical Trial Management

Full-service trial oversight from site selection through database lock, with dedicated cardiovascular project teams ensuring timelines and quality at every stage.

Regulatory Strategy

Navigate FDA, EMA, and global regulatory pathways with confidence. Our team crafts submission strategies tailored to cardiovascular therapeutic endpoints.

Cardiac Safety & Monitoring

Centralized ECG analysis, DSMB coordination, and cardiac adjudication with real-time monitoring dashboards built for cardiovascular risk assessment.

Biostatistics & Data Management

CDISC-compliant data pipelines and advanced statistical modeling for MACE endpoints, survival analysis, and adaptive cardiovascular trial designs.

Site Network & Patient Recruitment

Proprietary cardiovascular site network spanning 200+ clinical centers. Data-driven enrollment strategies to accelerate recruitment while meeting diversity goals.

Medical Affairs & Publications

KOL engagement, medical writing, and publication strategy ensuring your cardiovascular data reaches the right journals and clinical audiences.
