The Industry Problem

Every year, promising cardiac therapies fail — not because the science was wrong, but because the execution was.

80% of cardiovascular trials miss enrollment timelines. Protocol amendments delay programs by four months on average. Data quality issues surface at database lock, when fixing them costs ten times what prevention would have. CRO staff rotate off your trial mid-study, taking institutional knowledge with them.

You've seen senior scientists pitch your trial, then hand it to junior associates. You've watched budgets swell with change orders that weren't in the original scope. You've been told "partnership" by CROs that treat your program as one of hundreds.

We built CardioVera to solve those specific problems — not with slogans, but with operational commitments we publish and measure against.

What We Do

Specialized Cardiovascular Expertise

Clinical Operations

End-to-end cardiovascular trial execution with predictive enrollment analytics calibrated for cardiac indications. Indication-specific feasibility modeling across HFpEF, ACS, and ASCVD, with risk-based monitoring using centralized statistical signals and decentralized designs that maintain CVOT-grade rigor.

Phase I through pivotal • 40+ countries • Hub-and-spoke models

Data Management

Purpose-built for cardiovascular data complexity — ECG waveforms, imaging endpoints, and wearable biosensor streams converging into a single audit-ready database.

Biostatistics

MACE composites, win ratio methodology, ICH E9(R1) estimands, DSMB interim analyses, and submission-ready reports aligned with FDA and EMA cardiovascular guidance.

Pharmacovigilance

Cardiac safety surveillance across CiPA, ICH E14/S7B, arrhythmia coding, oncology cardiotoxicity, and GLP-1 MACE adjudication with SUSAR reporting.

Trial Design & Regulatory

Adaptive, event-driven, and Bayesian architectures grounded in FDA HF Endpoints and CVOT guidance. Breakthrough Therapy, Fast Track, and EMA PRIME pathways.

Core Lab Services

Blinded cardiac imaging across echo, CMR, CTA, and QCA. Integrated biomarker core for hs-troponin, NT-proBNP, with DICOM infrastructure and inter-reader monitoring.

Our Story

Built for Cardiovascular Complexity

Our founders — interventional cardiologists, heart failure trialists, and clinical operations leaders — saw the same pattern at large CROs: generalist teams rotating across therapeutic areas, unfamiliar with composite MACE adjudication or the rigor a 15,000-patient CVOT demands. They built CardioVera to end that pattern.

Our biometrics team is the strategic backbone of every program. Data management, biostatistics, and statistical programming operate as an integrated unit — not separate departments with handoff forms between them. The result: faster database locks, fewer post-lock issues, and submission packages that don't generate FDA information requests.

Cardiovascular trials are too consequential to be run by generalists.
