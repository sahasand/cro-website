[ WHAT WE DO ]

Core Capabilities

Cardiac Safety Monitoring

Real-time centralized ECG monitoring, QTc analysis, and cardiac event adjudication across global trial sites with 24/7 cardiologist oversight.

Endpoint Adjudication

Independent Clinical Events Committee management for MACE, heart failure hospitalization, and composite cardiovascular endpoint adjudication.

Phase 2-4 Trial Management

Full-service clinical trial management from protocol design through database lock, specialized in cardiovascular outcomes trials.

Regulatory Strategy

FDA and EMA cardiovascular regulatory pathway navigation, including REMS programs, adaptive trial designs, and breakthrough therapy designation support.

Site Network & Recruitment

Proprietary network of 800+ cardiology investigator sites across 40 countries, with AI-powered patient identification and enrollment optimization.

Biostatistics & Data Sciences

Advanced cardiovascular biostatistics including Bayesian adaptive designs, CVOT analysis, and real-world evidence integration for cardiac endpoints.

[ WHO WE ARE ]

Built for Cardiovascular Complexity

CardioVera Research was founded on a single premise: cardiovascular clinical trials demand a different kind of CRO. One that thinks in heartbeats, not just data points.

Our team of cardiologists, biostatisticians, and regulatory strategists has designed and executed over 150 cardiovascular studies spanning heart failure, arrhythmia, atherosclerosis, hypertension, and structural heart disease.

We operate across 40+ countries with a proprietary site network and have contributed to 23 successful cardiovascular drug and device approvals. Every decision we make is anchored in scientific rigor and patient safety.
