Who We Are

A Singular Focus on Cardiovascular Excellence

CardioVera Research was founded on the conviction that cardiovascular medicine demands a CRO with deep, specialized expertise rather than a generalist approach. Our team of cardiologists, biostatisticians, and clinical operations specialists brings unmatched domain knowledge to every study we support.

From Phase I first-in-human cardiac safety studies to pivotal Phase III cardiovascular outcomes trials (CVOTs), we engineer every aspect of the clinical development pathway. Our proprietary data infrastructure provides real-time analytics, adaptive monitoring, and predictive enrollment modeling that keeps trials on track and within budget.

We have supported regulatory submissions resulting in multiple FDA and EMA approvals across heart failure, atrial fibrillation, coronary artery disease, and cardiometabolic indications.
